01 — Who we are

A small studio with big ambition.

Bloxifo was founded on a simple idea: the best Roblox games come from teams that obsess over the player. Not the metrics. Not the trends. The player.

We're a tight-knit crew of creators, designers, and operators building original IP on the Roblox platform. Every system we ship is designed to feel fair, fun, and worth coming back to tomorrow.

We move fast. We argue loudly. We test everything. And we believe the next breakout game on Roblox is the one nobody saw coming.
